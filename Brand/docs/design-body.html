Foundations

Design system

Every product surface is built from these tokens. Indigo leads; the secondary palette appears only as brand expression (doc chrome, illustration, empty-state art) — never as a second accent competing with indigo in product chrome. Type is Inter only. Separation is done with 1px borders, not shadows.

Prototypes

Screen by screen

All 10 in-scope screens in priority order, each rendered inside a 375px phone frame. Checkout is #1 and most detailed — it owns the North Star of ≥40% conversion. Nothing out-of-scope appears: no KYC, wallet, storefront, edit-page, search, or multi-currency. NGN only.

1

Public buyer checkout

/p/:slug

Primary job

  • Carry a stranger from a shared link to a completed payment with zero hesitation. Owns the ≥40% North Star.
  • Chrome-free, single-column, one obvious action: Pay ₦X.

Key elements

  • Seller logo + business name + "Secure" cue in header.
  • Real product photo (or branded neutral state), title, muted description, large ₦ price.
  • Meta row: delivery + honest "★ N paid" (hidden at 0).
  • Collected fields (Name default), full-width indigo Pay button.

Trust & CRO (P0)

  • Real-photo over placeholder icon — the single biggest conversion lever.
  • "Payments processed by Paystack" line directly under the button.
  • Honest social proof ("24 paid") — hidden entirely at 0 rather than faked.
  • Tappable seller contact (WhatsApp / Instagram) lowers stranger risk.

Mobile @375px & a11y

  • Designed at 375px first; survives WhatsApp/IG in-app browsers.
  • Pay button 48px tall — exceeds 44px touch target.
  • Price uses real text (screen-reader friendly), not an image.
  • Photo carries descriptive alt; "Secure" pairs icon + text.

Paystack redirect interstitial

Primary job

  • Hold the buyer's confidence during the jump to Paystack's hosted page — the riskiest second in the funnel inside an in-app browser.

Trust & a11y

  • Calm spinner, explicit "Securely redirecting to Paystack…" copy, no dead-end if redirect stalls (fallback link).
  • aria-live="polite" status announces the redirect; spinner has reduced-motion fallback.

Receipt

/pay/receipt/:reference

Primary job

  • Confirm money moved and give the buyer something to keep — proof for a stranger transaction.
  • Lives at a stable reference URL that survives a closed tab (≥98% receipt-reach metric).

Key elements / states

  • Success: green check, amount, item, paid-by, reference, date; Download PDF + Share.
  • Failed: "payment didn't go through" with a clear Try again path back to checkout.
  • Secured-by-Paystack footer carries trust past the payment.

Create-page builder

/create

Primary job

  • Let a seller publish a payment page in under 90s with a live preview that removes doubt.

Key elements

  • Product/Service toggle, title, ₦ price, photo upload with real-photo nudge, description, delivery.
  • Buyer-field checkbox pills (Name default; Phone/Email/Address optional).
  • Live preview + success panel with Copy / QR / Share.

Mobile @375px

  • On mobile the live preview collapses above the form as a sticky thumbnail; desktop shows it side-by-side.

A11y

  • Every field has a real <label>; toggle and pills are keyboard-operable with visible focus.
